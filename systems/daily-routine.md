## Fitness Plan: Beginner Home Workout (No Equipment)

### Week 1-2: Foundation
**3x per week** (Mon/Wed/Fri or any 3 days with rest between)

**Warm-up (3 min)**
- Jumping jacks: 30 seconds
- Arm circles (forward + backward): 30 seconds each
- Hip rotations: 30 seconds
- High knees: 30 seconds

**Circuit (repeat 3x, rest 60s between rounds)**
1. Push-ups: 10 reps (knee push-ups if needed)
2. Bodyweight squats: 15 reps
3. Plank: 30 seconds
4. Reverse lunges: 10 each leg
5. Mountain climbers: 20 seconds

**Cool-down (3 min)**
- Quad stretch: 30 sec each leg
- Hamstring stretch: 30 sec each leg
- Chest/shoulder stretch: 30 sec
- Child's pose: 30 sec

**Total time: ~25 minutes**

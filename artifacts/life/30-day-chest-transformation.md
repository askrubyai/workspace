## Pillar 2: Diet Plan

### The Goal
**Caloric deficit** (burn more than you eat) while maintaining **protein** (preserve muscle).

### Daily Targets (Approximate)
- **Calories**: 1800-2000 kcal (moderate deficit)
- **Protein**: 100-120g (priority!)
- **Water**: 3+ liters

### What to Eat More Of

**High Protein (eat at every meal):**
- Eggs (6g protein each)
- Chicken breast (31g per 100g)
- Paneer (18g per 100g)
- Greek yogurt/Dahi (10g per 100g)
- Dal/Lentils (9g per 100g)
- Whey protein (if convenient)

**Fiber & Volume (fills you up):**
- Vegetables (unlimited)
- Salads before meals
- Fruits (moderate - high sugar)

**Real talk**: Smoking affects your transformation:

## Pillar 3: Lifestyle Hacks

### Immediate Bloat Reduction (Visible in 48-72 hours)

1. **Cut sodium drastically** for first week
   - No added salt
   - Avoid processed foods
   - No restaurant food if possible

2. **Increase water** (counterintuitive but works)
   - 3-4 liters per day
   - Body releases retained water when properly hydrated

3. **Reduce carbs temporarily** (first week only)
   - Carbs hold water (3g water per 1g carb)
   - Going lower-carb for a week can drop 2-3kg of water

### Posture (Instant Visual Improvement)

Bad posture makes chest look worse. Fix it:

1. **Shoulders back and down**
2. **Chin slightly tucked**
3. **Chest lifted**
4. **Wall checks**: Stand against wall, head/shoulders/butt touching

**Exercise**: Wall angels, 10 reps, 3x daily

### Sleep (Critical for Transformation)

- **Target**: 7-8 hours
- Poor sleep = higher cortisol = stubborn fat
- Sleep deprivation increases appetite
- Muscle recovery happens during sleep

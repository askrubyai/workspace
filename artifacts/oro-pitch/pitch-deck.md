# How It Works
## Dead Simple UX, Solana-Fast Execution

**Step 1: Connect with GRAIL**
Deposit GRAIL (Oro's 1:1 gold-backed token) or swap into it from SOL/USDC.

**Step 2: Browse Markets**
Find macro events you want to hedge: "Will gold hit $3000 by Q3?" / "Will CPI exceed 4% in March?" / "Will the Fed cut rates in 2026?"

**Step 3: Take a Position**
Buy YES or NO shares with your GRAIL. Positions are tokenized (ERC-1155 style), liquid, and tradable.

**Step 4: Settle in Gold**
When the market resolves, winners receive GRAIL. No slippage into USD. No taxable conversion events. Pure gold-denominated returns.

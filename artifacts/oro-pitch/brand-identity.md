## 4. VISUAL DIRECTION

### Color Palette: **Gilded Minimalism**

**Primary:**
- **Deep Gold** (#D4AF37 or #B8860B) ‚Äî Not bright yellow-gold, but aged, antique gold. Like old coins.
- **Obsidian Black** (#1A1A1A) ‚Äî Premium, serious, wealth-manager vibes. Not pure black.
- **Ivory White** (#FFFFF0) ‚Äî Slightly warm white, not stark. Complements gold without competing.

**Secondary/Accent:**
- **Sage Green** (#6B8E6B or #4A5D4A) ‚Äî Growth, stability, wealth. Subtle nod to traditional finance (old money energy)
- **Deep Indigo** (#1E3A5F) ‚Äî Trust, depth, night sky. For data viz and charts.
- **Copper Accent** (#B87333) ‚Äî Secondary metal, warmth, subtle contrast to gold without clashing.

**Usage:**
- Gold as accent, never background (keeps it precious, rare)
- Black/ivory as primary surface colors (90% of interfaces)
- Indigo for interactive elements (buttons, links, charts)
- Sage green for positive outcomes, copper for neutral states

### Typography: **Classical Modernity**

**Headlines/Display:**
- **Serif font** (Freight Display, Canela, Financier Display, or Tiempos Headline)
  - Why: Timeless, authoritative, gold/finance/old-world wealth associations
  - Evokes: Newspapers, legal documents, museum plaques
  - Weight: Medium to Bold, generous letter-spacing

**Body/UI:**
- **Geometric Sans** (S√∂hne, ABC Favorit, Inter, or Untitled Sans)
  - Why: Clean, modern, readable, crypto-native
  - Balances the classical serif with contemporary precision
  - Weight: Regular for body, Medium for labels

**Data/Numbers:**
- **Monospace** (JetBrains Mono, IBM Plex Mono, or S√∂hne Mono)
  - Why: Tabular data alignment, technical credibility
  - Use for: Price displays, prediction probabilities, timestamps

### Overall Mood: **Museum-Grade DeFi**

**Visual metaphors:**
- Ancient coins in acrylic displays (precious + transparent)
- Alchemical diagrams with clean, modern execution
- Brutalist architecture with gold inlays (structure + luxury)
- Financial charts rendered like constellation maps (data + mythology)

**Photography/Imagery:**
- If using photos: Black-and-white with selective gold color pop
- Textures: Hammered metal, stone etchings, aged paper ‚Äî all digitally refined
- Avoid: Generic crypto space imagery, lambos, rocket ships, cartoon illustrations

**UI Principles:**
- **Generous white space** ‚Äî Let information breathe. Confidence doesn't crowd.
- **Grid-based layouts** ‚Äî Mathematical precision. Nothing arbitrary.
- **Subtle animations** ‚Äî Smooth, physics-based. Gold shimmer on hover, but tasteful.
- **Data visualization** ‚Äî Clean, colorblind-accessible, annotation-rich (Tufte-inspired)

**Example Layout Vibe:**
- Stripe's clarity + FT.com's seriousness + Metalabel's elegance
- Dashboard feels like a private bank's trading terminal, not a casino
- Every pixel serves a purpose; every color carries meaning

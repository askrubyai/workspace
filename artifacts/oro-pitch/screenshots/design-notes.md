# Oro Finance — Design Aesthetic Notes

## Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: Deep forest green / dark olive (#1a2e1a or similar)
- **Secondary Background**: Warm cream/beige (#f5f0e0) on marketing site
- **Accent Gold**: Rich gold (#c9a84c) for buttons, highlights, icons
- **Text**: White on dark backgrounds, dark on light backgrounds
- **Success/Active States**: Green (#4CAF50-ish)
- **Cards/UI Elements**: Slightly lighter dark green with gold borders

## Typography
- **Headlines**: Serif font (elegant, traditional feel — matches gold/premium positioning)
- **Body/UI**: Monospace/technical for numbers and data, sans-serif for descriptions
- **Hero text**: "Gold. Just Better." — Large, bold serif

## Design Language
- **Luxury meets crypto**: Premium feel of traditional gold + modern DeFi interface
- **Dark mode dominant**: App is fully dark with green base
- **Marketing site**: Cream/beige with dark cards — editorial/magazine feel
- **Iconography**: Custom gold-tinted 3D icons (vault, treasure chest, coins)
- **Trust signals prominent**: "1:1 Vaulted Reserves", "Secure Custody (Brinks)", "Monthly Proof of Reserves (RSM)", "Bankruptcy-Remote Design"

## Key UI Patterns
- Simple swap interface (USDC ↔ GOLD)
- Staking with time-lock options (1mo/6mo/12mo)
- DeFi via Jupiter/Meteora integration
- "Each Gold Token Is 1:1 Backed By Real, Vaulted Gold" — trust banner on every page

## Brand Positioning
- Tagline: "Gold. Just Better."
- Sub: "Institutional-grade digital gold, earning 3-4% APY"
- Trust: LBMA/UAE GD-certified, Brinks custody, RSM auditor
- Partners: Brinks, RSM, Pyth, Cantina

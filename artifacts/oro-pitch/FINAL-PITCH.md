# 1. THE NAME: OROBOROS

**Oroboros** *(oh-ROH-boh-ros)* ‚Äî a portmanteau of **Oro** (gold, and the company) + **Ouroboros** (the ancient symbol of a snake eating its own tail).

### Why This Name Wins

The ouroboros is the perfect metaphor for what we're building:
- **Self-referential hedging**: A prediction market that settles in the very asset it predicts ‚Äî gold hedging gold, in an infinite loop
- **Oro brand synergy**: Maintains the "Oro" prefix, instant association with Oro Finance
- **Mythological gravitas**: Used across Egyptian, Greek, Indian, and Norse mythology ‚Äî timeless, like gold itself
- **Instantly visual**: The ouroboros is iconic. Works as a logo at any size.
- **Memorable**: Unusual but pronounceable. Stands out in a sea of "-swap" and "-fi" DeFi names.

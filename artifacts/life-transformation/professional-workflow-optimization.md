### 2. Role-Specific Workflow Systems

#### Treppa Engineering (Primary Income)
**Optimization Focus**: Maximum efficiency for core technical work

**Systems**:
- **Sprint Planning**: Weekly technical goals aligned with funding milestones
- **Code Review Batching**: Dedicated review blocks vs constant interruptions
- **Documentation Templates**: Standardized engineering decision records
- **Stakeholder Communication**: Scheduled update blocks (not reactive)

**Key Insight**: Engineering requires deep focus - protect 4-hour uninterrupted blocks

#### Podcast Executive Producer
**Optimization Focus**: Systematic production workflow

**Systems**:
- **Pre-Production Templates**: Guest briefing, content outlines, technical setup
- **Recording Day Protocols**: Equipment checks, backup procedures, post-recording tasks
- **Post-Production Pipeline**: Editing workflow, publishing schedule, promotion tasks
- **Guest Management CRM**: Contact tracking, follow-up sequences

**Key Tools**: ClickUp/Asana for production management, Trello for guest pipeline

#### Talent Scout  
**Optimization Focus**: Efficient identification and outreach systems

**Systems**:
- **Prospect Research Templates**: Standardized evaluation criteria
- **Outreach Automation**: Email templates, LinkedIn sequences  
- **Pipeline Management**: CRM for prospect tracking and follow-up
- **Evaluation Rubrics**: Quick decision frameworks for talent assessment

### 3. Energy Management Strategy

**Role Sequencing Based on Cognitive Load**:
1. **Morning (High Energy)**: Treppa engineering deep work
2. **Midday (Medium Energy)**: Podcast production and coordination
3. **Afternoon (Lower Energy)**: Talent scouting and administrative tasks

**Context Switching Minimization**:
- **Theme Days**: Monday/Tuesday (Engineering), Wednesday/Thursday (Production), Friday (Scouting)
- **Batch Similar Tasks**: All outreach on same day, all technical reviews together
- **Transition Rituals**: 15-min reset between role switches

**Delegation Candidates**:

### 5. Health Integration Protocol

**Critical Insight**: Professional efficiency supports life transformation goals

**Systems Integration**:
- **Morning Routine**: 20-min workout BEFORE first work block (energy boost)
- **Lunch Breaks**: Mandatory 45-min walk (anxiety reduction + physical activity)
- **Evening Shutdown**: Hard stop at 8 PM for sleep schedule optimization
- **Weekend Protection**: No work calls/emails (recovery time)

**Stress Management**:
- **Role Clarity**: Clear boundaries prevent bleeding between responsibilities  
- **Progress Tracking**: Weekly wins celebration for each role
- **Burnout Prevention**: Maximum 50 hours/week total across all roles
